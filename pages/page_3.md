The probability density function (PDF) of a normal distribution, also known as a Gaussian distribution, is given by the following formula:

$$
f(x) = \frac{1}{\sigma\sqrt{2\pi}} e^{-\frac{(x-\mu)^2}{2\sigma^2}}
$$
